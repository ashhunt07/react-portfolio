Lucky Bastard Company - Hand Crafted Lip Balm

This California based company creates hand crafted lip balm for the adventurous man. Unlike other lip balms, this is an essential for any travelerâ€™s bag. Lucky Bastard brand is not your run-of-the-mill lip balm. The brand promise is to stand up to even the harshest environments.

The goal was to create a cohesive brand and look across all products to promote brand loyalty. Currently there are multiple brands marking various products and areas of the site, so the most recent mark was taken and cleaned it up.

Package It

Original Lip Balm

No new products were created for Lucky Bastard Co. However, their three original products have been completely redesigned to fit with their new brand personality. These three lip balms are the brand ambassadors and the first products that were produced so a new look was crucial.

Other Products

The remainder of their products remained relatively unchanged with only minor updates to the logo or adding color to existing package designs.
